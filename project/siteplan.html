Site Plan: Popayán - Culture, Traditions, and Daily Life

Site Name

Popayán: Culture, Traditions, and Daily Life

I chose this name because it clearly represents the purpose of the website. The site focuses on showing the cultural identity, traditions, daily life, and historical importance of the city of Popayán, Colombia.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to present cultural, historical, and practical information about the city of Popayán, Colombia. The site will provide content about the history of the city, local traditions, gastronomy, important places and landmarks, festivals and celebrations, and useful information for visitors. The website will also include interactive sections using JavaScript to create a dynamic user experience.

Scenarios

  • "What are the most important traditions and festivals in Popayán?"
  • "Which typical foods should I try when visiting Popayán?"
  • "What places and landmarks are most important to visit in the city?"
  • "Where can I find basic information for visitors traveling to Popayán?"

Color Schema

Primary Color: #1f3c88 (Deep blue) - used for headers, navigation, and main accents.

Secondary Color: #f4f6ff (Soft light background) - used for section backgrounds and page background.

Accent Color: #f4c430 (Warm gold) - used for highlights, icons, and details.

Typography

Main Font: Roboto

  • Headings: Roboto Bold
  • Body Text: Roboto Regular
